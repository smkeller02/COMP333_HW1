What is the difference between Public and Private Accounts?

On MusicUnited, you have the option to set your account as either Private or Public.
When you set your account to Private, your music ratings and Music Personality are only visible to you and your friends.
If you choose to make your account Public, your music ratings and preferences are shared with any MusicUnited users.
